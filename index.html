About Me

My name is Selena Robins. I am an academic support administrator studying Software Development at BYU-Pathway Worldwide. My career objective is to develop scalable systems that support greater efficiency, transparency, and collaboration across different groups in higher education.

I have successfully helped develop a new grading system that handles thousands of submissions across three institutions of higher learning. I also recently developed a multi-language system to handle course maintenance tickets, creating automated flows to improve accountability and reporting across multiple programs. I am known for my innovative mindset, and my ability to address complex business processes with simple technical solutions.

I was born in Niagara Falls, Ontario, Canada to a Filipino mother and a Dutch father. I am married and have five children, 4 boys and 1 girl. My oldest son is married and my daughter is on a mission in Kentucky speaking Spanish. The other three boys are at home. In my spare time I like to spend time with my family and watch movies.
